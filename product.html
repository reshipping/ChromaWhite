The Science Behind ChromaWhite

ChromaWhite uses advanced color theory to whiten your teeth. Our proprietary purple formula contains carefully balanced violet pigments that sit opposite yellow on the color wheel. When applied to your teeth, these purple tones neutralize yellow staining, instantly revealing a brighter, whiter appearance.
